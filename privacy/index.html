Privacy Policy

Thanks for visiting isaacd2.com — home of Fickle Friday and a few other fun projects down the road. I want to be transparent about what information is collected and how it’s used.

1. What I Collect (and Why)

When you interact with this site (like subscribing to Fickle Friday or submitting forms), I may collect:

Your email address – so I can send you the weekly Fickle Friday newsletter or updates you’ve signed up for.

Your name (if provided) – to personalize things a bit.

Timestamps and IP address – for analytics and spam prevention, stored securely and not used to track you.

All of this is handled through serverless tech (AWS Lambda) and securely stored in Amazon DynamoDB.

2. What I Don’t Do

I don’t sell or share your data.

I don’t use invasive trackers or targeted advertising outside of industry standards.

I don’t collect sensitive info like payment details or personal IDs.

3. How Your Data Is Protected

I use Amazon Web Services (AWS), which provides top-notch security practices. Only I (Isaac) have access to the systems handling your data, and I take that responsibility seriously.

4. Opting Out

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in each email. If you ever want your info deleted completely, just send me a note at hi@isaacd2.com — I’ll take care of it.

5. Changes to This Policy

If I ever change how your data is handled, I’ll update this page.

If you have any questions, just reach out. I’m a real person behind this site, and I’m happy to help.

Keep experimenting,
